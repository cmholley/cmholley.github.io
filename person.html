In fact, I loved music so much that I audition for the 2013 San Antonio Crossmen, a World Class competitive DCI Drump and Buggle Corps. Unfortunately I was unable to finish the audition process due to a back injury, but I kept playing. Club Theater

Club Theater is an organization of students at the University of Houston who love the craft of acting and producing original work but who are not theater majors. Each semester, the group puts on between 7-10 short plays that are entirely written, directed, performed, produced and teched by students. I first joined Club Theater as a Sophomore and I am still currently involved. Enjoy my performance in Fall 2015's "The Protagonist of Reality"
